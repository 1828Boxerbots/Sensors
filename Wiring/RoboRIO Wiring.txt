A Navx is connected to the MXP port. On the Navx I2C port, 
we have connected Lidar wires.

Wires from Lidar

Red Pin1 to Navx I2C + pin
Pin 6 GND to Navx I2C GND Pin
Pin 4 SCL to Navx I2C SCL Pin
Pin 5 SDA to Navx I2C SDA Pin
